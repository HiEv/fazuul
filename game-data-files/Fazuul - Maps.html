Moving around the world of Fazuul is generally straightforward, but in some cases can have unusual results. Most of the game is built on a grid, but sometimes the linking between rooms seem off. If I start in a room, move 1 north, 1 west, 1 south and 1 east, normally I will end up at the room I started in, however, this is not always true in the world of Fazuul.

The most important item you can create for navigating the world of Fazuul will be the ceeveese. Looking at this item will show you the room # of the location you are currently standing in. This is extremely helpful when exploring the jungle outside the city. Room numbers are given on the maps [when available].

There are two methods of traveling to a different level of the city. First, located in the southwest corner of the town is an elevator. It moves automatically every minute or so, between the first and second floors. The second method of transport is the egg transporter. By placing an item bearing the inscription "Eggs are forever" in the transporter, you can move to different areas (I'm calling them "floors") of the city.
